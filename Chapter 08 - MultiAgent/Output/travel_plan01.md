### **DAILY SCHEDULE (Pace: Moderate)**

#### **DAY 1: Arrival & Cultural Immersion**
- **9:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Visit **Senso-ji Temple**
  - Explore Tokyo's oldest temple and traditional Nakamise shopping street.

- **12:00 PM - 2:00 PM:** **Lunch** at a nearby local restaurant (try tempura or soba).

- **2:00 PM - 5:00 PM:** Enjoy a **Kabuki Theater** performance
  - Immerse in traditional Japanese theater; check the schedule for shows.

- **5:00 PM - 7:00 PM:** **Dinner** (consider a local izakaya for authentic dining).

- **7:00 PM - 9:00 PM:** Participate in a **sushi-making workshop**
  - Learn the art of sushi-making and enjoy your creations.

---

#### **DAY 2: Culinary Delights**
- **9:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Attend a **ramen-making workshop**
  - Discover how to create delicious ramen.

- **12:00 PM - 1:30 PM:** **Lunch** (savor the ramen you made or visit a local ramen joint).

- **1:30 PM - 4:00 PM:** Free time for exploring local neighborhoods (perhaps visit nearby markets or parks).

- **4:00 PM - 7:00 PM:** **Dinner** in the Akihabara area, experiencing the vibrant culture.

---

#### **DAY 3: Further Exploration**
- **Morning:** Visit **Tsukiji Outer Market** for fresh seafood and local delicacies.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the **Ginza** area for shopping and upscale dining.

---

#### **DAY 4: A Taste of Tokyo's Nightlife**
- Spend the evening exploring **Shinjuku**, soaking in its vibrant nightlife.
- Visit **Shibuya** to see the famous crossing and Hachiko statue.

**Dinner recommendations:** Enjoy fusion Japanese cuisines or visit a themed cafe for a fun experience.

---

#### **DAY 5: Departure**
- Last-minute shopping or relaxing at the hotel.
- Flight back to Los Angeles, bringing along wonderful memories.
